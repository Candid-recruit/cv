As a career specialist I think each generation approaches work differently, and their outlook is heavily shaped by the economic, social, and technological forces of their time. From my experience I have found that this generation values the job flexibility that freelance work brings both in terms of work life balance but also exposure to different types of work which maybe more stimulating than a traditional 9-5. I really like their approach when it comes to not staying in a job if they are not fulfilled or feeling happy – why stay in a job if you are not happy and the organisation will not change? – life is after all to short to be spending most of your day feeling unfulfilled.
